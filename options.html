Teamwork Chrome Extension - Options

Overview:

This extension inserts a signature into your Teamwork.com comments.

Directions:

  • In order to insert a signature, press "ctrl+i" or click the extension icon in your toolbar.
  • You can use HTML to format your text. Here are a few tips:
    • Wrapping your text in <b></b> will make it bold.
    • Wrapping your text in <i></i> will make it italic.
    • Additional formatting syntax can be found here: https://www.w3schools.com/html/html_formatting.asp
